This 9-day intensive course introduces students to the core concepts of environmental economics, including externalities, public goods, valuation methods, and policy instruments.

For every module, we follow the following structure: * Discussion based on homework (videos or podcasts) * Lecture * Practice Handout * In-class Problem Set Note: on both Fridays, there will be a exam. The grade breakdown is: * Problem Sets: 40% * Exam 1: 25% * Exam 2: 25% * Participation: 10%

Click on each day to expand the materials.
